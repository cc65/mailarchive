Please remember that the list does only accept mails from subscribed mail
addresses. If you need to have your mail address changed, just unsubscribe
with the old, the re-subscribe with the new address.

Having a closed list was thought to be a measure against spam, but it seems to
cause confusion, since some people have more than one email address and they
usually have a reason for it. I will watch it for a while and then decide if
it's worth to change the policy.
