Hi All

I've just joined this list after rediscovering the joys of my boyhood days
of programming small systems (i.e. c=64).

I'd be interested to learn what plans there are for the further development
of the cc65 packages. (I noticed the 'roadmap' link on the cc65 web page
doesn't point to anything.)  

I'm interested in compilers and the linking/loading stage and I've also
written ELF and COFF linker/loaders for a previous product I worked on.  
I've made a brief start on writing cc65 versions of the Unix 'nm' and 'dump'
tools which I often find useful at various stages of development.  If
anyone's interested I'd be happy to make them available when I'm a little
further forward.

I'm prepared to take on technical work that might be required - only, of
course, if outside help is what is wanted.
