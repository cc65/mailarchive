I have registered the cc65.org domain yesterday, today it is active and
operational. The cc65 web pages are now available under http://www.cc65.org/.
The old address will continue to work for some time. I will change the
addresses of the mailing list in the near future, the cc65 packages will
probably stay on the musoftware FTP server.

This does also mean that I'm able to set up email aliases with a cc65.org
address. One may dispute the usefulness of such email aliases, especially,
since mail is routed through my permanent internet connection, which means
that I have to pay for it, but it looks nice:-) So if you have helped with the
development of cc65 and would like to have an email alias set up, please
contact me by email (you may use my new uz_at_cc65.org email address;-).
