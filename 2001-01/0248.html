Hi Troy,

>for(;((c=*value)!=39||(c=*value)!=62);)
>{
>	printf("%d",c);
>}
>
The loop condition is not correct, it must be:

for(;((c=*value)!=39 && (c=*value)!=62);) {...}

The condition fails when either *value == 39
or *value == 62, so you could write it also:

for(;!((c=*value)==39 || (c=*value)==62);) {...}
