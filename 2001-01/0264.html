I tried ccs64, and while the emulation seems quite good, the Win32 versions
is a little buggy. It works just fine in win9x, but in NT or Win2k it has
some glitches. It shows e.g. two shades of black in fullscreen.....

Personally I prefer Vice because :
- open source, well maintained (read : (2), partially thanks to (1))
- cross platform, meaning that whatever platform I will be running in 10
years, I'll be able to run vice. Ccs64 with it's asm code will probably
never leave the x86 world. (although in 10 years we'll run PC emulators on
our 256 bit CPU powered mobiles, to run our old x86 programs ..... ;-) )
- multiple machines (mostly I use it to emulate a c64, but it's fun to fire
up a PET or a VIC now & then :-)
