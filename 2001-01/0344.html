> CMD FD? :)  So, are you interested in sharing the source for this

actually, it's not my loader.... you can find the full sourcecode at
www.thedreams.de (click your way through 'sources' => 'dreamload' or sth)

source says it works with:

1541,1571,1581,CMD Harddrive,CMD FD,CMD RamLink,CMD RamDrive

> Did you come up with one generic routine that runs on all the
> different drives, or did you have to seperate code for some of
> them?

its a generic routine for 1541/1571, one for 1581 and one that works
with CMD devices...

the only thing missing for 100% perfection is, that it currently doesnt work
on NTSC systems. (oh and a little flaw in the original source is that the
option to load under i/o was removed for speed increase, you can always mod
the source to do it though)
