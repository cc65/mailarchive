> It works fine with junkbuster, so I don't think it's the Referer. I
> just tried with Javascript and cookies disabled and with junkbuster

You made me curious. I've done some tests and you are correct. It's not
junkbuster that is causing the trouble, but the line

	anonymize_headers deny From Referer Server

in the squid proxy configuration. Since the "Referer" header does not cause
the problem, it must be the "From" or "Server" header. (Not that this has
anything to do with cc65:-)
