> > up with loads of dead code linked in. ... mmmmh ... sounds to me as if having

> 
> How about
> 
> #include <graphics.h>
> #include <graph160.h>
> 
> for 160x200.
>

> 
> #include <graphics.h>
> #include <graphall.h>

Another technique I have seen used is something like this:

#define GR_NEED GR_NEED_ALL
#include <graphics.h>

or 

#define GR_NEED (GR_NEED_160 | GR_NEED_480)
#include <graphics.h>
