> 
> I and all of my family have been completely shocked by this act of terrorism.

On behalf of the American People, thank you both.

Right now it is hard to think about these things. My manager at work
has a niece who worked in the World Trade Center in New York City, on
the 100th floor of the second tower to be attacked. Miraculously, she
was able to escape. I cannot describe our feelings as we saw the towers
collapsing. I wept. I also cannot describe our feelings when we saw
people rejoicing in the streets. A deep burning anger has been aroused
in the American People. I do not believe that diplomacy and the justice
system will be used to right this wrong, probably the single greatest
murder ever in the history of the human race. This was an act of war,
and will be dealt with as such, I hope and believe.

I am sorry that events cause my emotions and desire to express them
overwhelm my desire not to post off-topic.
