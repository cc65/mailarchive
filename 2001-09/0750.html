And use what instead ? command.com ? It's probably a better idea
to write makefiles for sh and let Cygwin and DJGPP take care of
the DOS/Windows problem than to maintain two sets of makefiles
in parallel.

that it runs Cygwin) is more productive than changing cc65 so
that it doesn't need Cygwin. No need to torment Mr. von Bass
Joke. :-)
