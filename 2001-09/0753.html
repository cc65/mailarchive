i have finally found some sparetime to put this stuff i have always
been talking about together ,) just dont expect too much, this is all
at a real alpha state...maybe useful though if used with caution.

in the zip are:
- filesystem library, includes most lowlevel standard C stuff,
cbm-style routines and some turbo-c alike things
- a few additional console i/o funcs
- simple irq handler
- fixed version of the commandline-parser
- some little sample/test progs i wrote...dont take them too seriously
;=D

ftp://ftp.musoftware.de/pub/groepaz/cc65snapshot1.zip  (121kb)

if you just wanna try the libs w/o recompiling or anything, just copy
the contents of /include/c/ to your /include/ dir, and all .lib files
from /lib/c/ to your /lib/ directory..... for those who want to play
with the sources i included my environment settings etc aswell, just
have a look ;=) oh and for those without a bit of patience, there are
precompiled .d64's of all samples in /samples/ aswell ;=P

have fun :o)
