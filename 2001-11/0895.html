>         AtasciiToANTIC .strat(Arg, I)
>         .endrep

"AtasciiToANTIC .strat(Arg, I)" is a call of "AtasciiToANTIC" macro with two
arguments: ".strat(Arg" and "I)". You should call it with the result of
.strat(Arg, I):

    .repeat .strlen(Arg), I
        .proc
            Ch = .strat(Arg, I)
            AtasciiToANTIC Ch
        .endproc
    .endrep
