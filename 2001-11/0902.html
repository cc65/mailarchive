There's a simple optimization that could be applied in many places in cc65
and runtime lib:

Before:
 sta tmp1
 lda something
 sbc tmp1

After:
 eor #$FF
 adc something

Of course, provided that tmp1 doesn't matter.

BTW. There's a bug in libsrc/runtime/sub.s: after .ifpc02 there should be
lda (sp).
