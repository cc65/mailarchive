> AD> As far as I know, Windows has SLIP support.

I asked a question about SLIP support in Windows on the lwIP mailing list, 
and got the answer that it indeed was supported - "...create a new dial up
connection; select direct cable connection between two computers; then,
under networking, select SLIP as the type of dial-up server."

The whole answer is here: http://www.sics.se/~adam/lwip/maillist/msg00204.html
