Wednesday, April 17, 2002, 11:50:24 AM, you wrote:

MM> On Wed, 17 Apr 2002, groepaz wrote:

>> think of a system with scpu, AR and REU for example.....

MM> How would you access the 8k RAM on the Action Replay and the REU
MM> simultaneously?  The DMA controller of the REU sits at $dfxx, which is one
MM> window to the Action Replay memory.  Several years ago, I made a small
MM> hardware modification that allowed a REU and an AR to be plugged in
MM> simultaneously.  I made the REU registers write-only, so that they didn't
MM> interfere with the Action Replay, and I think I added a switch for
MM> disabling the REU altogether.  By enabling 256 bytes of the RAM bank of
MM> the Action Replay at $df00-$dfff, I was able to access the REU.
MM> Obviously, the AR RAM locations that were "underneath" the REU registers
MM> were overwritten.  If you are interested in the details, search for
MM> "actionreuplay" in the prlink or cbmlink source code.
