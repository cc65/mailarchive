and just to make it a complete answer...

in textmode, the highnibble of $d018 selects the 1k block from where
screen data is fetched, starting from 0 in 1k increments. the
lownibble does the same for charset, but in 2k increments.
the lower 2 bits of $dd00 select the 16k block from where the vic can
fetch data, 3 means $0000, 0 $c000 here.
