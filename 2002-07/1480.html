MV> Blinking led is the result, and reading the error channel gives me
MV> 73, jiffydos 6.0 1571, 18, 00. WTF?

MM> Maybe you have toasted the DOS version byte in the beginning of
MM> track 18, sector 0, in your previous tries? Then the DOS would

that the data written gets shifted by one byte. 18,00 starts with 00
21 01 41. A B-P: 2 0 fixed that problem. I though B-P weren't neces-
sary for U1/U2? At least that's what the 1541 and 1581 user guides
claim.
