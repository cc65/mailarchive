Is the bank-switching problem already solved in CC65 ? 8-bit Ataris now
have 1 MB RAM or 1 MB cartridges, but this memory is available through 16k
window. As long as CC65 uses 16-bit pointers, it has limited scalability.
In 2600 and 5200 consoles there's much less memory, but still it's often
bank-switched (e.g. 4k ROM pages for 2600).
