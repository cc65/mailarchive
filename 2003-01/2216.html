> (to Uz)
> Looking at lunix.cfg for ld65, I see that ZEROPAGE segment
> begins at $80 -- are zero-page locations in o65 put with
> $80+offset_in_zp_segment; or, just offset_in_zp_segment,
> and it is up to loader to relocate them using zp base-address from header?
> Current loader for LUnix does not relocate zp (it is silently assumed that
> base-address of zp is 0) because applications have to have access
> to system-data in $02-$80 range.

It is $80+segment_offset -- as you said before, relocation isn't needed.

"cc65/asminc/lunix/system.inc" defines system-variables with address-literals.
They aren't affected by the loader.

I need o65_loader to be a public function (defined in "jumptab.h").
Then, the LUnix version of mod_load() can use it to do most of the work.
