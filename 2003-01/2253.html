> and use _only _'buffer1' anywhere else in my source,
> also buffer2 is reserved and linked? - regardless of
> the usage?

Yes. Linking is always done based on modules. The reason is that the linker
does not know if there are references to buffer2 inside the module. I had once
thought about a feature to split modules into independent submodules, so the
linker could discard unused submodules. But since this feature would bring
anything new, and is a lot of work, I decided against it.

So, if you want to have two buffers which are only linked in when actually
used, you will have create two modules.
