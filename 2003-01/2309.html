Here is a more compact source version of his code:
--------------------------- buffer.s ----------------------------

        .export _buffer

BUFSIZE         = 1024
BUFALIGN        = 4 - 1         ; Double-word alignment

        .bss
buf:    .res    BUFSIZE + BUFALIGN

_buffer = (buf + BUFALIGN) & ~BUFALIGN
