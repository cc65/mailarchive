When I experimented with command-lines, I made a little "test suite."  I
wrote a program that did nothing but return a zero error-code.  I put that
"cc65" and the real cl65 into a directory.  I linked the names "ca65" and
"ld65" to "cc65".  (Make copies if your system doesn't have links.)  I used
the debug option (-d) to make cl65 show the commands that it creates.
