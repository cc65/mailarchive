I came up with a very simple workaround that should work for all
drives:

  cbm_open(1, 8, 0, "$");
  cbm_close(1);
  cbm_open(1, 8, 2, "$");
  /* do stuff */
  cbm_close(1);

That dummy open on channel 0 seems to reinitialize the drive, and then
the second open works OK.
