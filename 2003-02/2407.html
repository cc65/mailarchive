> kann sich einer vorstellen, warum folgendes programm nicht funzt ?

There are two problems with your code:

> void  setcharcolors(ROYWINCOL_T colors);

cc65 does not allow to pass structures by value. This is explicitly stated in
the docs:

    C Functions may not return structs (or unions), and structs may not be
    passed as parameters by value. However, struct assignment *is* possible.

>  	setcharcolors({1,0});

This is not valid C according to the C89 standard.
