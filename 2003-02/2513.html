ok...i am right now uploading this to the ftp... the package contains both the 
source for tetris, prebuild binaries for all targets, and subdirectories with 
a bunch of stuff that probably could all go into the libraries (startup for 
nes/pce, conio for nes/pce,joystick support for nes/pce some more additional 
conio,soft80-driver for c64,soft40 driver for vic20,joystick drivers for the 
4 player interfaces and rs232 for the silversurfer (which is obviously 
unsused in tetris but i dropped it in there anyway :=P))

the current state of tetris itself is pretty stable. the nes and pce stuff 
must be incorporated into the library (not linked againt some none.lib) and 
some glitches in the additional conio stuff should be fixed (atari and apple 
etc)...also some stuff that probably should better go into drivers/modules is 
currently linked statically. (nes/pce joystick driver, rs232)

well, and a good bunch of files still has wrong copyright info in them :=P

mmmh....i guess the additional conio stuff should be discussed (also look at 
my todo list for some more ideas) but i'm to tired now to write it all 
down...perhaps you have a look ullrich and tell me what you think :=P 

oh and btw... the cbm2 ports have weirdo problems.... even without the 
routines i added (some might be terribly wrong) its crashing in vice 
1.11...argls (it worked in earlier versions). maybe you can check that on the 
real thing :)

oh and if there is some basic wizard around...have a look at 65tris.bas and 
fix it for plus4/c16 :=P
