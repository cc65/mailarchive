> 68K-style suffixes? Add lda.w and lda.l that only accept 16 or 24-bit

Or operand prefixes:
	lda z:0    ; 8-bit address
	lda a:0    ; 16-bit
	lda l:0    ; 24-bit
z: and a: are supported by xasm, but I think I've seen them in other
assemblers, too.
