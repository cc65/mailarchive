>
> C:\My Programs\cc65

>
> "C:\My Programs\cc65\bin\utod.exe" C:\My Programs\cc65\run.bat
>
> The utod program would try to convert two files:
>
> C:\My
> Programs\cc65\run.bat
>
> The second pair of quotation-marks will prevent that mistake.
