Have you tried the Interactive Disassembler (IDA) by Datarescue.
I find this great for reverse engineering. I bought the full thing
a number of years back but there is a freeware (DOS) version that
does the same stuff (6502 wise). Search for idafw.zip - Simtel & 
mirrors usually have it. There is a newer freeware version at
http://www.themel.com/ida.html but I haven't tried this myself.
