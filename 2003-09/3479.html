>>>>> "UvB" == Ullrich von Bassewitz <uz_at_musoftware.de> writes:

UvB> Can you send me a short description of the drivers, so I have
UvB> something to add to the c64 platform docs? I have to admit that
UvB> I've never heard of a "DXS/HIT-4 Player"

This is the Digital Excess & Hitmen adapter. It's just two DSUBs
connected to PB0-PB7, SP2, and PA2 on the userport. You can get the
instructions on how to build it here:

  http://www.digitalexcess.de/downloads/productions.php

UvB> and "PTV-4 Player" joyticks before.

This is the Protovision 4-player adapter sold here:

  http://www.protovision-online.de/hardw/hardwstart.htm

I built one of these myself, and it's great for 3-player IK+ or
Rampage, and 4-player Bombmania.

They both do pretty much the same thing - give you two more joystick
ports connected to the user port.
