> checked the BSD TCP/IP stack bible yesterday (TCP/IP Illustrated volume

packet(7) is your friend (at least on Linux).

socket_type = SOCK_RAW really gives you the whole LLC frame, while
socket_type = SOCK_DGRAM removes the LLC header. 

So, SOCK_RAW is exactly what is needed.
