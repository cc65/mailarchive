On Tue, Oct 28, 2003 at 04:14:17PM -0600, Geoffrey Wossum wrote:
> Shouldn't that be (__PARMSTACK_START__ + __PARMSTACK_SIZE__ - 1)?

No. The stack pointer points to the last value pushed, it is decremented
before something is stored. This is different from the hardware stack pointer
of the 6502, which does the store first and then the decrement.
