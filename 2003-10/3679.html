Hello.

I think I discovered a small flaw, yet again, within DA65. :( It seems to
disassemble the actual indirect Y ($00),y addressing mode instructions as
indirect X ($00,x) addressing mode instructions.

Most indirect Y addressing mode instructions were disassembled properly,
but quite a few were disassembled as indirect X addressing mode
instructions.
