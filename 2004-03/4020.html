Dennis Kuschel has sent me a small demo for his "pico]OS" RTOS that runs on

        ftp://ftp.musoftware.de/pub/uz/cc65/contrib/picoos-demo-0.8.0.txt
        ftp://ftp.musoftware.de/pub/uz/cc65/contrib/picoos-demo-0.8.0.zip

information about pico]OS:

        http://sourceforge.net/projects/picoos/
