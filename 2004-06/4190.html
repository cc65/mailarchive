you might use the C-style define:

ch_enter=13
.define chendter ch_enter,0
.byte "hallo", chendter

results in
0000000: 48 41 4c 4c 4f 0d 00                             HALLO..

cheers,
Stephan

>
> .macro chendter
> CH_ENTER, 0
