> Is it supposed to be
>
>  asm("ldy %o", ret);
>
> or
>
>  asm("ldy #%o", ret);
>
> (does "%o" supply the "#")?

You are right! The '#' is missing. The %o does not supply the '#', so the
correct statement is

        asm ("ldy #%o", ret);
