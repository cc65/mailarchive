with the compiler that came with exactly these samples, and not with the one,
that happens to lie around in /usr/bin. I know that this could be argued,
there's also a valid reason for using the compiler from /usr/bin. But just
changing the paths from one to the other is no real solution. Either we can
have both possibilities, or we need two makefiles:-)
