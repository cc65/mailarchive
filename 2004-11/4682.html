I would like to use KDevelop. It highligts the C-code correctly
and it can call make with the target name. That means that compiling
and linking works just fine.

find a makefile. I have not found what goes wrong yet.

The reason I like KDevelop is that it is free, integrates well with Gimp
for graphics creation and it has a great editor.
