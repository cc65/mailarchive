On Fri, Jul 29, 2005 at 07:01:59PM -0400, Greg King wrote:
> What is your current opinion about the joystick API's use of a default
> driver filename (extern const char joy_stddrv[])?

The difference between the joystick and a graphics driver is that all
platforms have exactly one standard joystick interface (the one built
into the machine itself). But most systems have more than one graphics
mode, and it's difficult to define, which one is "the standard". This is
the reason why the approach won't work very well for tgi drivers.
