>>>>> "RW" == Raj Wurttemberg <rajw@c64.us> writes:

RW> How to I do the chr$(x) with cbm_write? Is this correct?

RW> cbm_write(15, "m-w\x06\x00\x02\x18\x00", 8);

Yep. I used a struct in my code:

  struct mwcmd {
    char mw[3];
    void *addr;
    unsigned char len;
    char data[34];
  };

  struct mwcmd mw;

  memcpy(mw.mw, "M-W", 3);
  mw.addr = (void *)0x0006;
  mw.len = 2;
  memcpy(mw.data, "\x18\x00", 2);

  cbm_write(15, &mw, 8);
