I see those error messages, alot.  They are accurate, but they don't help
us to understand what we did wrong (I would like to see a different
message).  The bug usually is one of these:

1) a typo or misspelling,
2) an undefined macro.

When ca65 can't recognize the first word on a line, it thinks that we
wanted to write a label (which needs a colon).  That "add" macro must be
defined by adding this line next to the ".P816" line:

    .macpack    generic
