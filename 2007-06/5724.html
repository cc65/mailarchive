> 01-BOOT-1000.s boot-0-3000.s boot-2-b000.s subs.s 08-MUSI-9a00.s
> boot-1-b800.s"

That is because .SECONDARY doesn't recognize the pattern-wildcard "%".
So, ".SECONDARY: %.s" looks for one particular file
with the three-character name "%.s".
