Hello,

I was wondering if it would be possible without too much effort to  
support a syntax like:

.export CHROUT = $FFD2

instead of

.export CHROUT
CHROUT = $FFD2

It would IMHO make writing some files much easier and less error prone.

The second thing is that I noticed in the packaged include files,  
namely kernal.s of c64 subdir of libsrc that sevaral functions are  
named differently than in both of the major resources like "C64 PRG"  
and "Mapping C64". Of course it doesn't affect the cc65 package at all  
but a global search and replace on the related files would bring  
consistency towards those two books and on the way make life easier  
for people who would like to reuse the include files rather than  
writing their own from scratch. Nothing really important but a small  
nit to pick.. What do you think?
