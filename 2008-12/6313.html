On Saturday 29 November 2008, Tachdaun wrote:

>  PS: I'm aware of PEEK, in fact it inspired addr(x), but I use it in
> place of POKE, for example: addr( 0x4016 ) = 1; instead of POKE(
> 0x4016, 1 );
>  It just looks better for me.

With constants it looks IMHO even better if you give them names, like::

 #define FOO	((unsigned char*)0x4016)

And then ``FOO = 1;`` instead of ``POKE(0x4016, 1);``
