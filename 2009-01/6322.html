> How do I use DOS 3.3 or ProDOS functions in a cc65 program for the Apple 
> 2?

DOS 3.3: No supported: http://www.cc65.org/snapshot-doc/apple2-6.html

ProDOS 8: Use the POSIX functions (open, read, write, close, fopen, fread, 
fwrite, fclose, ...).
