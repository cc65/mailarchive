See "C style macros" at http://www.cc65.org/doc/ca65-11.html#ss11.7

The following works:
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
.define setup_bg1(map,char) 0+((map .SHR 12) .OR (char .SHR 8))

        lda     setup_bg1($2000,$00)
        sta     $1234
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
