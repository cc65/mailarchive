from C labels.  Particularly when JSRs are involved:

    asm("jsr %g", downward);
    return;

downward:
    asm("lda #$00");
    asm("rts");
    return;

I can only assume that jsr wont work this way in the inline assembly - only
jmp and other branch opcodes.
