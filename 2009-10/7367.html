The Atari 8-bit has quite a few strange modes, with various different color
registers that can be used.  I haven't really looked at the TGI specs up to
this point, but I don't think it makes sense to try to force all the Atari
modes into some convoluted TGI paradigm.  In my opinion, a few modes should
be implemented for cross-compatibility, basically only ANTIC mode E 160x192,
4 colors) and F (320x192 monochrome).

Most anything serious won't use the TGI drivers anyway.
