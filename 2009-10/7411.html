It all depends on how exactly your display device is adjusted. If
you take the standard 4x3 display and put 640x256 pixels in it you
effectively have pixels sizes of 160:85.33333, significantly under
2:1 (comes out at 1.875:1).
 
However, placing a ruler on my monitor shows 640 horizontal pixels
displayed in 230mm and 256 vertical pixels in 184mm, giving pixels
sizes of (230/640):(184/256) = 0.35:0.71 = 1:2 - but's that's
because I've adjusted my monitor to ensure square pixels.
