Some targets may support some built-in compressed image formats. To save 
space it would be good to support them.

The Lynx has basically two formats:
- uncompressed literary image where every pixel is defined
- compressed image that is a kind of a runlength encoded format

The literal format is typically only used on images that have dynamic 
content or on single pixel images.
