Beware: There's a secret hidden bug in all cc65 versions since - I don't know,
but probably for at least 10 years: If you jump out of a local scope that
declares variables using goto, the stackpointer is not adjusted and the
program will crash. Using break or continue is ok, but I would avoid these
constructs with cc65 anyway.
