First, I want to say thank you to all the folks in here who've helped me
work through some interesting challenges the last couple of weeks.  I'm sure
many of you think I'm daft and I'm quite sure you're correct.  But, out of
that madness has been born a project that I hope will grow some legs and
support.

Introducing CBM-Command, a Norton Commander like disk manager for the C64
and C128 computers.  The home page for it is located at: <
http://cbmcommand.codeplex.com/>.  Please take a look.  It's completely open
source (BSD license) and the site has a D64 with both the C64 and C128
versions.  Any and all feedback is greatly appreciated!
