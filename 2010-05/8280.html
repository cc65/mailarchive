Given the amount of thoughts and work evested into making cc65
cross-6502-target I think it's sort of a pity that CBM-Command isn't
called 6502-Command (as example) and isn't designed to run on i.e.
Apple2 machines too.

Even if it would require some #ifdef's in example because there's no
opendir() in the CBM C library it should be portable to other cc65
targets with reasonable effort. And now that we have a fully
functional mouse driver for the C64 even mouse support is easily
doable in a cross-target fashion...

Just my two cents,
