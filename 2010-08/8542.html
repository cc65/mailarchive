Hi,

Breadbox64 - a Twitter Client for the C64 - is the first third-party
application based on cc65-Contiki. It was created by Johan Van den
Brande:
http://www.vandenbrande.com/wp/2009/06/breadbox64-a-twitter-client-for-the-c64/
http://contikiprojects.svn.sourceforge.net/viewvc/contikiprojects/vandenbrande.com/twitter/

Now I ported Breadbox64 to the C128 (VDC) and the Apple II using
Contiki 2.4 and cc65 2.13:
http://img299.imageshack.us/img299/3060/c64.png
http://img826.imageshack.us/img826/1697/c128.png
http://img37.imageshack.us/img37/6062/apple2enh.png

Maybe this success story motivates someone to make Breadbox64 the
first, but not the only third-party Contiki application by creating
his own...

Regards,
Oliver
