On Fri, Aug 06, 2010 at 01:08:05PM +0200, Carlos wrote:
> but when i link i get the error: "ld65.exe: Error: Missing memory
> area assignment for segment `EXTZP'". I have to change the linker
> configuration file to include this new segment?

Yes. Just have a look into the linker config for these platforms to see how it
is done. The built-in linker configurations are in

        src/ld65/cfg
    
Grab a copy of the one for your platform and make the necessary changes. Then,
instead of passing "-t platform" to the linker, you specify "-C myconfig".
