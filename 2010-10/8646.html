On Fri, Oct 01, 2010 at 06:18:10PM +0200, Oliver Schmidt wrote:
> I just noticed that by far not every cc65 target defines the macro
> CH_ENTER. Is this macro indeed optional or should cc65 apps be allowed
> to rely on its exsistence?

I didn't deliberate such things for each single macro when writing the API and
first implementations. In addition, some of the implementations are done by
peoples who weren't "real" users of the machines in questions. I for example
have written the Oric support without ever having touched one of those
machines.

So if you need more definitions, the best idea would probably be to just add
them. Since existing platforms are often used as templates when writing
support for new ones, the new macros will automatically propagate.
