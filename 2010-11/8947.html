I think you could pack the word list down from 10k in some way, unless
you've already done this.  You could pack 63 different characters (plenty
because you probably only need 26+space and maybe a few punctuations) in 6
bits, giving you 4 letters per 3 bytes.  Or 31 in 5 bits, giving you 8
letters in 5 bytes.

 

4 letters per 3 bytes = 3k saved (approx).

8 letters per 5 bytes = 3.75k saved (approx.)

 

This is something like the way the Z-machine packs its text.

http://www.gnelson.demon.co.uk/zspec/sect03.html

 

I suspect that there are more clever ways to do this.
