Hi Greg,

> I am unhappy with the April 20th patch to <tgi.h>.

Sorry for commiting without first explaining !

> I believe that TGI's black and white colors should be defined globally.
> They should be 0 and 1 on _all_ platforms -- they shouldn't be
> target-specific.  On targets where the hardware uses different numbers,
> the graphics drivers should remap the numbers.

As Uz already explained the cc65-way-of-doing-things is to push as
much effort as reasonable from runtime to designtime. A nice example
are the macros for switching between 40 and 80 column display:

apple2enh:
#define VIDEOMODE_40x24 0x0011
#define VIDEOMODE_80x24 0x0012
#define VIDEOMODE_40COL VIDEOMODE_40x24
#define VIDEOMODE_80COL VIDEOMODE_80x24

c128:
#define VIDEOMODE_40x25         0x00
#define VIDEOMODE_80x25         0x80
#define VIDEOMODE_40COL         VIDEOMODE_40x25
#define VIDEOMODE_80COL         VIDEOMODE_80x25
