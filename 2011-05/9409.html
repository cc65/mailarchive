On 05/11/2011 02:33 PM, Oliver Schmidt wrote:
> Ideas, comments, opinions, objections?
>

 From the programmers point of view I would scrap the concept of 
TGI_COLOR_RED completely.

Instead I would have pens.

tgi_setpen(index, R, G, B);
tgi_setpen(index, intensity); // For bw display

To draw in black I could write:

tgi_setpen(0, 0);

White would be

tgi_setpen(1, 255);

Red could be

tgi_setpen(2, 255, 0, 0);
