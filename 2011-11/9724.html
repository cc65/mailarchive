The name "sd2iec" is used for two things: DOS firmware and a particular
one of the devices that use that firmware.  The convention is that
lower-case (sd2iec) means the firmware; and, upper-case (SD2IEC) means
the hardware.

I do the same thing with "CC65".  Upper-case means the entire package,
while lower-case (cc65) means the C-compiler component of the package.
