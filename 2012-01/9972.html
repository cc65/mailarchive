For those who don't know, Hidden64 is a cc65 library to allow access to the C64's 'hidden' RAM, providing up to 61k RAM plus the tape buffer and other unused space in the first 1k.  Memank128 is still being developed and is intended to give cc65 programmers for the C128 a Bank 0 configuration for 56k RAM, access to memory in the first 7k--including the 2.25k applicatio buffer--and usage of Bank 1 for data, totallig 118k+.  For Hidden64, I have in mind a hangman demo and for MemBank128, a brainstorm demo.  I am looking for other ideas.  If you have a idea for me, please tell me.  If you want to program one yourself, I ask you to tell me and post your demo on the cc65 contribs site.  MemBank128 should be out soon.
