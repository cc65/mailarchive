More info:
The location and size of the hole are dictated by hardware.  The
text/graphics part of the video chip cannot see RAM there.  It is
redirected to a font ROM, instead.  Because of that, demoes and games
usually don't put their graphics data there.  As others said,
it has become a tradition to fill that area with music.
