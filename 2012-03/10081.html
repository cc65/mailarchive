A cartridge is basically a simple dump of the ROM image.  The same
goes for the EXTROM or INTROM on the C128 -- I'm playing with that
myself for a kernal replacement.  There are some magic bytes at the
top of the ROM that identifies it as a cartridge to the kernal that
you'll need too.  Check out http://www.n2dvm.com/leif.html for more
info.
