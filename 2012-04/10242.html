> 9. Setting the -remotemonitoraddress to anything other than 6510 doesn't work?

The format is unique and tricky.

Try:

-remotemonitoraddress ipv4://127.0.0.1:6510

to bind to port 6510 on localhost only.

Try

-remotemonitoraddress ipv4://:6510

to bind to port 6510 on any IP address.

I hope you get the idea how it works. ;)
