For this to work (also in the macro) you will have to enclose the string into
a .PROC:

        .proc   M11
                asc     "2A"
        .endproc
                   
        .byte   .sizeof (M11)

Using .PROC introduces additional typing, so part of the advantages of .sizeof
are lost.
