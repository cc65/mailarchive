Another memory driver for the Atari 8-bit... uses the memory under the OS
ROM on XL and XE computers.

You can check it out here... source in the zip file.
http://www.atariage.com/forums/topic/202370-cc65-emd-driver/#entry2595299

It works, and passes the emtest program and works with Multidemo, but there
are few things that could be polished up... there's quite a bit of screen
flicker in the emtest, and I'm not sure if that can be avoided or not... I
have to turn off all NMI (VBI and DLI) during the memory copy, which may be
causing the flicker.
