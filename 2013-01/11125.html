I got it working.  I had to make fontData and fontIndex ZP variables and
load them with the appropriate addresses as such:

	lda #_ormand
	clc
	adc _ormand+font::IndexPtr

	sta fontIndex
	clc
	adc #$02
	sta fontNextIndex
	lda #_ormand
	clc
	adc _ormand+font::FontDataPtr
	sta fontData


Then I had to change the references in my code from lda fontIndex,y to lda
(fontIndex),y.
